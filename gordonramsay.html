GORDON RAMSAY DINING

Gordon Ramsay's Bread Street Kitchen And Bar

Experience the best of Britain at Gordon Ramsay's restaurant in Dubai. Inspired by the original London warehouse setting, Bread Street Kitchen & Bar is a favourite destination for families and friends, featuring outdoor dining and a Saturday family brunch.

Live entertainment every Saturday and Sunday afternoons.

On the menu, expect a modern take on British classics, such as fish and chips in a craft beer batter and the legendary beef Wellington. At the bar, pick from a broad range of wines, beers, and cocktails, including Bread Street's signature gin and tonics.
