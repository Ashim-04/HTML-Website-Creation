NOBU DINING

Nobu Dubai

Nobu restaurant, located on the iconic Palm Jumeirah, has been selected as one of the best restaurants in Dubai by the MICHELIN Guide. It offers a world-renowned Japanese cuisine with a Peruvian twist for a culinary experience like no other. Begin your night with cocktails and Japanese tapas in the sake lounge , before sitting down for dinner to taste an exceptional omakase or a la carte menu in the dream-like Japanese garden. Indulge in celebrity chef Nobu Matsuhisa's iconic black cod miso, premium wagyu beef tacos with a Japanese twist, and Dubai's best sushi.

Meet The Chef

Nobuyuki Matsuhisa known to the world
simply as "Nobu" is the acclaimed and highly influential chef
of Nobu and Matsuhisa restaurants. After working in Japan
for many years, he moved to Lima before heading to Los Angeles to open his own restaurant in Beverly Hills.
The restaurant quickly became a hot spot and was frequented by Hollywood
celebrities, including Robert De Niro, who invited Nobu to set up more
restaurants around the world. Today Nobu has 50 restaurants, 13 hotels around the
world and holds 5 Michelin Stars.
